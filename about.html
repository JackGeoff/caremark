Who We Are

Caremark Hospital Limited is a licensed private healthcare facility offering comprehensive outpatient medical and specialist services. We are dedicated to providing timely, affordable, and ethical healthcare in a warm and professional environment. Our facility features modern consultation rooms, a fully equipped diagnostic laboratory, a licensed pharmacy, and dedicated spaces for physiotherapy, counselling, and nutrition services. At Caremark, we take time to listen, explain, and treat because every patient matters.
