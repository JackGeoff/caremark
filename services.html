Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions across multiple specialties

General Outpatient Services

General Medical Consultation

Comprehensive health assessments, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of medical conditions by experienced general practitioners.

Preventive Health Screening

Proactive health checks and early disease detection to keep you healthy and identify potential health risks before they become serious

Minor Procedures and Wound Care

Professional treatment for minor injuries, wound management, suturing, and minor surgical procedures in a safe clinical environment.

Immunization and Family Health Services

Complete vaccination services for all ages, family planning, maternal health support, and wellness programs for the whole family.

Health Education and Lifestyle Counselling

Personalized guidance on healthy living, disease prevention, chronic condition management, and lifestyle modification.

Specialist Clinics

Internal Medicine

Expert diagnosis and management of adult diseases including diabetes, hypertension, and chronic illnesses.

Obstetrics and Gynecology

Comprehensive women's health services including prenatal care, family planning, and gynecological consultations.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly, compassionate environment.

ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

Diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the ear, nose, throat, and related structures.

Dermatology

Expert skin care including treatment of skin conditions, allergies, infections, and cosmetic dermatology.

Oncology

Specialized cancer care, consultation, and management with access to oncology medications.

Nutrition and Dietetics

Professional dietary guidance for weight management, chronic disease control, and optimal nutrition.
